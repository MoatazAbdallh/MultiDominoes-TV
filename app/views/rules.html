Game Initialization
* The game begins by shuffling the dominoes and dealing a hand to each player.
* Each Player starts with 7 cards.
* The player with the highest double makes the first move.
Gameplay
* The play proceeds and each next player adds a matching domino to an open end of the board.
* In case the player cannot play, he should draw from the boneyard.
* When the boneyard cards have been finished, player should pass his turn.
* The round continues until one player runs out of dominoes,or no more plays can be made.
Scoring
* The player with the lowest hand wins the round.
* If there is a tie,the winner is the player with the lowest individual domino.
* The winner receives the sum of all points in other player's hands minus the sum of points in his own.
