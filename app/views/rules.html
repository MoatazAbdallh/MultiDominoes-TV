How to Play Dominoes

Setup

  • The game begins by shuffling the dominoes and dealing a hand to each player.
  • Each Player starts with 7 dominoes.
  • The player with the highest double makes the first move.

Gameplay

  • The first player can start by playing any domino from their hand.
  • The play proceeds and each next player adds a matching domino to an open end of the board.
  • In case the player cannot move,they must draw from the boneyard until they receive a domino that they can play.
  • A player who cannot make a move and cannot draw because the boneyard is empty must pass their turn.
  • The round continues until one player runs out of dominoes,or no more moves can be made.

Scoring

  • The player with the lighest hand(least number of dots on their remaining dominoes) wins the round.
  • If there is a tie,the winner is the player with the lightest individual domino.
  • The winner receives the sum of all points in their opponent's hands,minus the sum of points in their own hand and the next round begins.
